Golden Brown CornBread

This cornbread recipe came from my grandma. My mom started making this cornbread years ago when I was a kid. We use the recipe only in special occasions like Christmas and Thanksgiving. It's completely homemade with a soft inside, a golden crust, and a rich flavor. What makes this cornbread even better is that it can be enjoyed with many stuff like barbecue, soup, and mac & cheese but best of all with honey. I find this cornbread to be the best cornbread I have ever had.

Cornbread Ingredients

The following Ingredients are basic and cheap ingredients. Here's what you wil need:

  • Flour: This cornbread recipe needs one cup of alpurpose flour.
  • Cornmeal: Try to get yellow cornmeal for this recipe because it's sweeter then the white one. (you can use the white one if you want)
  • Sugar: Use 2/3 of a cup sugar for the perfect amount of sweet.
  • Baking Powder: Baking Powder will help the cornbread rise.
  • Salt: A teaspoon of salt will enhance the flavors of other ingredients
  • Milk: Use Whole milk, 2% milk or any non-dairy milk this will help the batter have a better consistency.
  • Oil: Vegatable oil keeps the cornbread nice and moist.
  • Egg: One egg can bring moist and act like a binding agent.

What Goes with cornbread

Cornbread is one of the desserts that can go with many meals I personally like is with Mac & cheese and with barbecuebut you can pair it up with many more foods like chili, fried chicken, soup, and stew. These are just a few things cornbread goes with so there many options when it comes to cornbread.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 3 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1ice /3 cup veagtables
  • 1 large egg

Directions

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) Grease up a 9 inch round pan.
  3. Whisk flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, and salt all together.
  4. Add milk, vegatable oil, and egg whisk untill well combined.
  5. Pour batter into the pan
  6. Bake in the preheated oven and wait around 20-25minutes
  7. Slice it up and enjoy
